How do I play FlexCrop if DMU currency doesn't exist?

It's easy!

FlexCrop uses what is called The FlexCrop Standard (FCS for short) which contains multiple items with their own individual values. To get FCS, you simply must find it through the survival world. You can also act in trades between players to accumulate the currency if you are using FCS.
